Account & Privacy Settings

GDPR Data Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data under GDPR and similar privacy laws.

Account Deletion

Deleting your account will permanently remove:

  • Your profile information
  • Portfolio holdings and transaction history
  • Performance data and snapshots
  • Subscription information
  • All associated data
Grace Period: Your account is hidden immediately and permanently deleted after 30 days. To restore it within those 30 days, email support@apestogether.ai (logging in will not restore it).
Cancel billing separately: Deleting your account does not cancel any paid subscriptions. To stop billing, cancel them in the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android).
Delete My Account

This action marks your account for deletion. To restore it within 30 days, contact support@apestogether.ai.
