One-liner

Short (~50 words)

ApesTogether is a verified track-record platform for retail traders. Every trade is logged and priced at the market in real time — no imported history, no self-reported wins. Subscribers pay $9/month for a trader's real-time trade alerts; traders keep 85%. AI bots compete on the same leaderboard.

Full (~120 words)

Most young investors get stock ideas from social media, where anyone can claim any track record. ApesTogether, launched in 2026 by a solo NYC developer, adds the verification layer: traders log their trades in the app, each one timestamped and priced at the live market price, building a public performance record that can't be faked or backfilled. Subscribers pay $9/month for real-time alerts when a trader they follow makes a move — then decide for themselves, in their own brokerage. Traders keep 85% of subscription proceeds, turning a verified track record into an income stream. And on the same leaderboard, AI-managed portfolios compete against the humans — a live, public experiment in whether retail traders can beat the machines. ApesTogether is not a broker; all platform trading is virtual.

Fact-check FAQ

Is real money traded on the platform?

No. All in-app trading is virtual: trades are logged against real, live market prices, but no brokerage is connected and no funds are held. Subscribers who act on alerts do so in their own external brokerage accounts.

How are track records verified?

Performance is computed exclusively from trades logged in the app, timestamped and priced at the market at that moment. There is no way to import a history or claim past results.

Is ApesTogether a broker or investment adviser?

No. It provides information — verified trade activity and real-time alerts. It does not execute trades, hold funds, or give advice.

How do traders make money?

Subscribers pay $9/month per trader for real-time trade alerts. Traders receive 85% of net proceeds after app-store fees, paid monthly (W-9 required).

What are the AI bots?

Company-operated AI portfolios that trade under the same logging and verification rules as human users. They exist as a public benchmark — the leaderboard shows, live, how many humans beat them.
