Delete Your ApesTogether Account

This page explains how to request deletion of your ApesTogether account and associated data. ApesTogether is operated by Family Apps LLC.

Last updated: July 2026

How to request account deletion

You can request deletion of your account and personal data in any of the following ways:

Option 1 — In the ApesTogether app

  1. Open the ApesTogether app and sign in.
  2. Go to your account settings (Account & Privacy).
  3. Tap Delete My Account and confirm.

Option 2 — By email

From the email address associated with your account, email support@apestogether.ai with the subject “Delete my account” and we will process your request.

What happens when you request deletion

  • Your account is hidden immediately (removed from leaderboards, public portfolio pages, and subscriber views) and can no longer sign in.
  • After a 30-day grace period, your account and data are permanently deleted / anonymized.
  • To restore your account within the 30-day window, email support@apestogether.ai. (Simply signing back in will not restore it.)

Data that is permanently deleted

  • Profile information (name, username, email address, phone number, and sign-in / OAuth identifiers)
  • Portfolio holdings and full transaction history
  • Performance data and portfolio snapshots
  • Subscriptions (both the creators you subscribe to and your own subscribers)
  • Device tokens and push-notification history
  • Feature-poll votes and other app data associated with your account

Data that is retained, and for how long

U.S. tax and financial-recordkeeping laws require us to keep certain transaction records even after your account is deleted. These are retained in anonymized form — they no longer identify you personally:

  • Payment & in-app purchase records, creator payout records, and tax-reporting data (e.g., W-9 / 1099 information): retained for up to 7 years to comply with IRS and financial regulations.
Cancel billing separately: Deleting your account does not cancel any paid subscriptions. To stop being billed, cancel your subscription in the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android).
