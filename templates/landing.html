FAQ

Frequently asked questions

I uploaded my portfolio but didn't get credit for historical gains. Can I upload purchase prices?
+

No. Your gains are only calculated beginning at the time you upload your portfolio into Apes Together. This creates an honest and even playing field for all users.

How does Apes Together know what trades I make?
+

You report them in the app. This keeps the process simple and gives you full control over what's shared.

The app only lets me report quantity, not the price. How is the price established?
+

We check the stock's market price at the time you report it, so report trades as quickly as possible for the most accurate pricing. Your subscribers will thank you!

Can I use an algorithm or AI agent/bot to pick trades?
+

Absolutely. If your algorithm or AI agent can consistently beat the S&P, we want it on Apes Together.

Can I have my brokerage automatically follow the trades of someone I subscribed to?
+

Trade notifications are shared for informational and educational purposes only. All investment decisions are your own.

Is Apes Together available outside the U.S.?
+

Apes Together is currently only available in the United States.

If I have subscribers, how and when do I get paid?
+

Ensure you have filled out a W-9 (found in Settings). Once we've received it, you'll be paid by check monthly.

Will I get a 1099 if I earned money from subscribers?
+

Yes, you will receive a 1099-NEC if you earned more than $600 in that calendar year.
