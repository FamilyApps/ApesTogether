| App | Title | Subtitle | Rating | Reviews | Key Differentiator |

| **Webull** | Webull: Investing & Trading | Stocks, ETFs, Options | 4.7 | 700K | Advanced charting, paper trading |

### Key Observations
- **Title pattern**: `Brand: Action Phrase` (e.g., "dub: Copy Real Investors")
- **Subtitle pattern**: Short value prop, 3-6 words max
- **All competitors** use "Invest" or "Trade" in either title or subtitle
- **Social/copy trading** is the hottest niche — Autopilot, dub, eToro all lead with it
- **None** of the top competitors use "paper trading" or "virtual" in their title/subtitle
- **Our advantage**: No real money needed, no brokerage account, social competition + leaderboards

## Recommended App Store Listing (iOS)

### Title (30 char max)
```
Apes Together: Social Trading
```
**Rationale**: Brand + primary keyword "Social Trading." Matches the naming pattern of top competitors (Brand: Action). "Social Trading" has high search volume in finance category.

### Subtitle (30 char max)
```
Paper Trade & Compete for Cash
```
**Rationale**: Hits 3 high-value keywords: "Paper Trade," "Compete," "Cash." Communicates the unique value prop — it's risk-free but with real prizes. This differentiates from every competitor since none combine paper trading with cash prizes.

### Alternative Subtitles (A/B Test)
- `Virtual Stock Trading & Prizes` (emphasizes risk-free + prizes)
- `Copy Top Traders. Win Prizes.` (emphasizes social copy trading + prizes)
- `Trade Stocks Risk-Free & Win` (emphasizes no-risk + winning)

### Keywords Field (100 chars, comma-separated, no spaces)
```
stock,investing,portfolio,leaderboard,copy trading,virtual,stocks,market,simulator,trade,finance,compete,prizes,paper trading,social investing
```

**Strategy**: Mix of high-volume (stock, investing, portfolio, finance) + medium-competition long-tail (copy trading, paper trading, social investing, leaderboard, compete) + unique differentiators (prizes, virtual, simulator).

  - **7**: "Beautiful Dark Mode" — settings or alternate theme view
  - **8**: "4,000+ Real Stocks" — stock search/browse screen

### Screenshot Design Guidelines
- **Background**: Dark gradient (matches app's dark mode aesthetic)
- **Device frame**: Latest iPhone 16 Pro mockup
- **Font**: SF Pro Display or Inter Bold, white text
- **Accent colors**: Green (#34C759) for gains, app brand colors
- **No small text**: Headlines readable at thumbnail size
- **No feature lists**: One message per screenshot

## In-App Events (iOS) / LiveOps (Google Play)

Use seasonal events to boost visibility:
- **"New Year Trading Challenge"** — January (new users setting financial goals)
- **"March Madness Portfolio Bracket"** — March (cultural moment)
- **"Summer Trading Sprint"** — June (students out of school)
- **"Q4 Earnings Season Challenge"** — October (earnings season buzz)

These appear in the Today tab and search results, giving free promotional placement.

## Ratings & Reviews Strategy

- **In-app prompt**: After 3rd successful trade (user is engaged, feeling accomplished)
- **Never prompt**: After a loss, after an error, or within first session
- **Review response**: Respond to every 1-3 star review within 24 hours
- **Goal**: 4.7+ rating before launch marketing push

## Localization Priority

1. **English (US)** — primary market
2. **English (UK)** — same language, different locale (free extra indexing on iOS)
3. **Spanish** — large US demographic
4. **French (Canada)** — Canadian market

iOS tip: Each locale gets its own Keywords field, effectively multiplying your keyword capacity by the number of locales you support.
