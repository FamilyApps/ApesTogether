# INFLUENCER OUTREACH

## Tiering Strategy

**Tier 1 (Day 12+):** Large creators. Unlikely to respond to cold outreach but worth trying. Low conversion expectation.
**Tier 2 (Day 8+):** Mid-size, niche creators. Best ROI — large enough to matter, small enough to engage.
**Tier 3 (Day 4+):** Micro-creators and active community members. Highest conversion rate. Start here.

## General Approach
1. **First contact:** Short, personalized DM or email. Under 100 words.
2. **Follow-up (1 week later if no reply):** Even shorter. One sentence + link.
3. **Never send a third message.** If they don't reply twice, move on.
4. **Always offer something:** Beta access, exclusive feature, data for content.
5. **Never ask them to "promote" you.** Ask them to try it and share their honest opinion.
